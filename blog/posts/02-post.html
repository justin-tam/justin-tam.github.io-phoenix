Vision and Values

We focus our real estate investment strategy on acquiring properties in growing markets that show improving demographic trends, and target properties in amenable locations to ensure the viability of the real estate location for the tenants. Our acquisitions team uncovers opportunities consistent with this strategy. Our research department uses its underwriting expertise to assess tenants' long-term prospects beforehand and then we seek to maintain tenant, industry, and geographic diversification through our acquisitions and portfoli management strategies.

Our relationships with high net worth investors and commercial banks allow us to continually grow and build on our successes, while our conservative balance sheet keeps our costs low and offers financial stability through various economic cycles. At Phoenix Imperial, we treat money seriously. No dollar is misspent, no penny is unaccounted for. Efficiency, growth, and transparency are how we operate. We invite and encourage our investors to review our annual reports so that they may better understand how we operate.

Why now?

2020 has taught us that nothing is guaranteed in life. Whether it's hurricanes, wildfires, pandemics, economic shutdowns, riots, or any other number of natural and man-made disasters, this year has shown us that we cannot predict the future. With this in mind, the importance of diversity and long-term stability have never been more necessary for our financial health. That's why we formed Phoenix Imperial. To provide a safer, growth oriented alternative to the volatility of the market, and a more reliable source of income than government bonds and 401(k)s.
