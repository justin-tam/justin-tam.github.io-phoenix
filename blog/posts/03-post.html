Growing Wealth into the Future

Why now? And what's next?

Why now?

2020 has taught us that nothing is guaranteed in life. Whether it's hurricanes, wildfires, pandemics, economic shutdowns, riots, or any other number of natural and man-made disasters, this year has shown us that we cannot predict the future. With this in mind, the importance of diversity and long-term stability have never been more necessary for our financial health. That's why we formed Phoenix Imperial. To provide a safer, growth oriented alternative to the volatility of the market, and a more reliable source of income than government bonds and 401(k)s.

If steady income through real estate is something you'd like to hear more about, reach out to us via our contact form. If you're still on the fence, read on to learn more about how Phoenix Imperial can help you take that extra vacation, save for retirement, expand your nest egg, and perhaps become a real estate mogul.

What's Next?

This year (2020) is about vetting investors and determining whether we have the social support and financial foundation to grow faster than we have in the past few years. Assuming a successful investment campaign, Phoenix Imperial intends to incorporate as a Limited Liability Company to better take advantage of legal, financial, and tax benefits as we execute our portfolio acquisition strategy through 2021. Our goal is to deliver monthly dividends to our investors starting one year after initial investment and incrementally increasing in % return annually over a 5 or 10 year period based on the selected investment product.

If you read the prior two pieces, then you've learned about REITs and how they are great vehicles for real estate investments. Phoenix Imperial's plan of action moving forward consists of building up the necessary components to transition our organization into a REIT so that as we grow and expand, we may better pass those legal, financial, and tax benefits onto our investors. Until then, we here at Phoenix Imperial intend to continue serving our investors, our tenants, and our communities with the utmost respect and care as we grow our portfolio and stay vigilant for investment opportunities from sudden market shifts.
